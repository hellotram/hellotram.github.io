Grew up in Vietnam

Born and raised in a small highland town called Buon Ma Thuot, Vietnam, Tram came to the United States at the age of 17 with a dream of becoming something larger than her life.

Driven by art and science

She has always been into design, art, fashion, and human’s psychology. She yearns for implementing the beauty of art into every day’s reality and practicality. She has tried various types of jobs and interests, and landed with Graphic Design major as something she thought at the time the closest to her goals.

Started out as a

Graphic Designer

Having a Graphic Design bachelor degree and working in Visual Design industry, she had chances to practice design principles, eyes for colors and compositions. However, doing just visual aspects for a design somehow didn’t completely fulfill her.

“How could I make my design more impactful?”, she asked to herself then decided to expand into UX Design.

Began the journey of

User Experience

Taking a 10-week design immersive course at General Assembly, implementing different approaches, using data to back up for every design decision, Tram was ready to make a big jump into UX design. This website is the first step to bring her closer to the UX world.

Adventures and food exploring are her callings

Besides UX, she loves going on a new adventure, call it a new backpacking trip, vacation or exploring any hot food spot in town. As a Saggitarius at heart, she runs on fuel filled by these ventures. She would be more than happy to talk about locations, things to do and food at place she has and has not been to, with anyone.
